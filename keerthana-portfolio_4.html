My Approach

How I Design

UX design is the practice of creating products that are useful, usable, and delightful. My process is structured yet flexible — rooted in real user needs and business goals.

01 — DISCOVER
Research & Empathy
User interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis to deeply understand the problem space and real user needs.
02 — DEFINE
IA & User Flows
Synthesizing research into personas, user journeys, and clear information architecture before designing anything.
03 — DESIGN
Wireframe & UI
From low-fidelity wireframes to high-fidelity, pixel-perfect interfaces — using scalable design systems in Figma.
04 — VALIDATE
Test & Iterate
Usability testing, heuristic evaluation, and iteration based on real feedback — ensuring designs actually work.
